The Best Movies According to Olya

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Fifth Element (1997)

What it's about: In the colorful future, a cab driver unwittingly becomes the central figure in the search for a legendary cosmic weapon to keep Evil and Mr. Zorg at bay.

Why I like it: The most elaborate world, the most epic protagonist.

Gladiator (2000)

What it's about: A former Roman General sets out to exact vengeance against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family and sent him into slavery.

Why I like it: The most epic story, magnificent cimetography, one of the best original soundtracks.

Iron Man (2008)

What it's about: After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.

Why I like it: This movie once started a wonderful journey into the world of Marvel superheroes. And it's simply cool by itself!
