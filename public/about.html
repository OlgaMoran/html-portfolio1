About Me

Приветствую! My name is Olga, and I'm a Russian-born language enthusiast currently residing in the vibrant land of South Africa. With 22 years of experience as a technical editor in a scientific publishing house, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a passion for crafting clear, concise language. Now, I'm embarking on an exciting new chapter in my professional journey – web development.

My fascination with technology and the endless possibilities of the digital world has inspired me to pursue a career in web development. I'm eager to combine my linguistic expertise with technical skills to create engaging and user-friendly websites.

When I'm not immersed in the world of words and code, I enjoy spending time with my son, a young man of 25 who shares my love for cats, detective stories, and the soothing melodies of lo-fi music. These shared passions bring us closer and provide a welcome respite from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

As I delve into the dynamic realm of web development, I'm committed to bringing my meticulous approach, creative spirit, and lifelong love of learning to every project. I believe that technology, when wielded with care and purpose, can bridge gaps, foster understanding, and enrich our lives.

I'm excited to connect with you and explore the possibilities that lie ahead.
